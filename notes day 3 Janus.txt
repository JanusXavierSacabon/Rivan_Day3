Named EIGRP / Multiprotocol EIGRP

Formula:

[(10^7 * 65536) / Min BW ] + [Total Delay psec * 65536] / 10^6

From R4 to 10.2.1.0/24

Step 1: Find the Values
Min BW: 10000
Total Delay: 1000 + 10 = 1010

Step 2: Bandwidth / Throughput

= 10^7 * 655336 / 10000
= 655360000000/10000
=65536000

Step 3: Delay / Latency
=[Total Delay psec * 65536] / 10^6
= 1010 x10^6 /1 000 000
= 66 191 360

Step 4: Add BW and Delay then Divide via Rib Scale (128)
=(66 191 360 + 65536000 ) / 128
= 1029120
